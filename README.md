# Grooove

> Modern Music Player for Legacy iOS

## Status

🚧 Under Active Development

## Vision

Grooove is an open-source modern music player designed for legacy iOS devices.

Instead of relying on obsolete streaming clients, Groooove connects directly to modern self-hosted music servers such as Navidrome.

## Planned Features

- 🎵 Navidrome Support
- 💿 Vinyl-inspired Now Playing
- 🎨 Dynamic Album Colors
- 📚 Album Browser
- 🔍 Search
- ❤️ Favorites
- 📀 Local Music Support (Planned)
- 🎧 Jellyfin Support (Planned)

## Target Devices

- iOS 8
- iOS 9
- iOS 10

Minimum deployment target: **iOS 8.0**. iOS 7 is out of scope — see [docs/PLATFORM.md](docs/PLATFORM.md).
